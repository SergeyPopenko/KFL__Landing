Our process

  • 1

    Discovery

    Understanding client's requirements.

    Business goals.

    Who is target. audience assessing competition.

  • 2

    Planning

    Choosing what technologies should be implemented.

    Creating site map.

    Determining the timeframe.

  • 3

    Design

    Creating the prototype designs.

    Alignment of details.

    Arriving at the final design for web site.

  • 4

    Development

    Writing valid HTML / CSS code.

    Implementing CMS.

    Creating the actual, functional site.

  • 5

    Deployment

    Launch the site.

    Address feedback from users.

  • 6

    After launch

    Constant care and maintenance.

    Updating content.

    Making changes to the backend and fixing broken links.
