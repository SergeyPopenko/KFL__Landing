Our process

  • 1

    Discovery

    Understanding client's requirements.

    Business goals.

    Who is a target.

    Audience assessing competition.

  • 2

    Planning

    Choosing what technologies should be implemented.

    Creating a site map.

    Determining the timeframe.

  • 3

    Design

    Creating the prototype designs.

    Alignment of details.

    Arriving at the final design for a website.

  • 4

    Development

    Writing valid HTML / CSS code.

    Implementing CMS.

    Creating the actual, functional site.

  • 5

    QA&Testing

    Testing all functions and appearance on multiple browser platforms.

    Preparating to go live website.

  • 6

    Launch

    Launching the site; addressing feedback from users.

    Repairing bugs.
